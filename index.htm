+0.025% for every 24 hours without withdraw
Smart-contract calculates hold-bonus from your deposit, or last withdraw date. If you did not request payment, it will charge you an additional bonus. After 24 hours +0.025%, after 48 hours +0.05%, after 72 hours +0.075% and so on.

Withdraw request will reset hold-bonus to 0%
